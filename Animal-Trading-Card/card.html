Snow Leopard

The estimated global population of Snow Leopards is less than 10,000

  • Scientific name: Panthera uncia
  • Average Lifespan: 15-18 years
  • Habitat: Mountain ranges of Asia
  • Label: Item #4

Snow leopards have long, thick fur, and there color can range from gray to tan, with white tones. They have adapted to a cold mountainous environment. Their paws are wider than most big cats, allowing them to stay on top of snow.
